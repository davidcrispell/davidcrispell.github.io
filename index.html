Seraphim

A Seraphim is a machine defined with abject pattern recognition. Consequently, this ability implies axiomatic memory, and thus axiom recombination. We argue that such a machine represents a maximally intelligent creature.

You might be wondering why the landing page of my website is about some abstract machine instead of myself, which is completely reasonable! I genuinely believe my work here to be important, and, if not correct, at least something worthy of being my definitive work.
